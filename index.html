About Us

We SAM TRAVELS are specialised in Corporate and event travel management, delivering seamless, cost-effective travel solutions for businesses and organisers. From executive bookings to large-scale event logistics, our team handles everything so you can focus on your priorities.

  • Corporate & Employee Travel Management: policy creation, bookings, traveler tracking, expense integration, and custom reporting.
  • Event & Group Travel: multi-city itineraries, block hotel bookings, group check-ins, on-site coordination, and contingency planning.
  • VIP & Executive Services: curated itineraries, meet-and-greet, chauffeur transfers, and concierge-level support.
  • Compliance & Cost Control: vendor negotiation, preferred supplier programs, and audit-ready reporting.

Our Mission

To deliver reliable, scalable travel solutions that free our clients to focus on their core business — not travel logistics.
